# Task: OBS15-060 — Operational Access Policy + Audit

## Purpose

Define operational access as exceptional authority and ensure it is auditable (Domain AE).

## Implementation Notes

- Create an operational access policy:
  - who can access
  - what they can do
  - under what scope
  - time-bounded access
- Ensure operational actions are:
  - explicit
  - scope-bound
  - fully auditable

## Acceptance Criteria

- Ops access is documented and reviewable.
- Operational actions produce auditable records.
